Krunal Pandya

A year after they signed his younger brother Hardik, Mumbai Indians (MI) got Krunal Pandya in the IPL 2016 auction for INR 2 crore.
A spin-bowling allrounder, Krunal quickly became a key part of a strong lower-middle order at MI alongside Hardik and Kieron Pollard.
