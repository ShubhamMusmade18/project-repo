Josh Hazelwood

Josh Reginald Hazlewood (born 8 January 1991) is an Australian international cricketer.
He is a pace bowler known for his accuracy and has been compared to former Australian paceman Glenn McGrath.
He has captained Australia in a ODI and served as a Test vice-captain after the 2018 Australian ball-tampering scandal.
